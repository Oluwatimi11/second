Grill

Delicious recipes for your electric grill

It sounds too good to be true - a low-fat cooking technique
that reduces the time you need to spend in the kitchen and practically
guarantees success! But that's exactly what today's electric grill does.
Chops, steaks, burgers, chicken, fish, seafood, vegetables, and fruit
can be cooked to perfection in a matter of minutes. The grill
is fast approaching the top of tge list of most wanted -
and most useful - modern kitchen appliances.

The recipes in this book are designed to take full advantage of
this quick, easy and clean method of cooking and are simple to adapt
to suit the size of your grill and the size of your family. There is
something for every taste, occasion, season and course, from
Spicy pawns to Grilled Fruit with Papaya Sauce and freom Steak
Bearnaise to Polenta with Tomatoes & Garlic Sauce.
